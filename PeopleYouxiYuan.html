Youxi Yuan

Postdoctoral Research Associate

Youxi Yuan earned his PhD degree in plant molecular biology and genetics from Institute of Genetics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, China. His current research project is to dissect the biochemical mechanisms underlying the acetylation of xylan, one of the major components in plant biomass.
